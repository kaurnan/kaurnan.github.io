Library

Library facility is available for all the B. Tech. students.The students who become the members of the Central Library can get the books issued as per Institute rules.

The Central Library also boasts of a highly technological aid of the Digital Library Facility throughout the intranet network of the institute along with a dedicated digital library with 22 hi-tech multimedia terminals. In addition to this, Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC) facilities are also provided to all the users. The Library offers inter-library loan service to its users for the books and periodicals & other reading material currently not available in the institute Library by procuring them from other libraries through DELNET (Developing Library Network).

They can issue General Books, Books from Book Bank and Books from Overnight collection.
